Chapter 1: Overview

With the release of Monster Hunter world and me finding a Monster Manual for Monster Hunter,
I wanted to create a Monster Hunter inspired Campaign.

This Document will contain an overview of the world and how you would build a character inside this world.

The only Rule is thats this is my homebrew world, so it will differ significantly from the established world of Monster Hunter, if you at any point say: This isn't how this works in Monster Hunter,
you are subject to the possibility of me taking away a level.

Chapter 2: The World

The Corruption

Magic came in a flash and brought many new wonders with it.
Empires grew from and wars used this new power, but just as sudden as it began it disappeared.
In the absence of magic monsters poured out of the earth, feeding on everything and everyone magical.
Desperately defending, all of [Placeholdername] was forced into Strongholds, the last few bastions of survival.
With magic gone, few have retained the ability to cast spells (cantrips) and every now and then sparks of the arcane lend their light to those in need (spell slots),
others use monster parts to augment themselves (Sorcerers) or their machinery (Artificer or Bloodhunter).
Slowly this new confined life took the norm, with only a brave few obtaining the permission to venture out,
the monster hunters.
